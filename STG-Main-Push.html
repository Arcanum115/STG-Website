About STG

Welcome to the Special Tactics Group (STG), an elite Arma 3 unit focused on realistic military operations and tactical gameplay. Our unit emphasizes teamwork, communication, and authentic combat scenarios.

Founded in 2020, STG has grown into a tight-knit community of dedicated players who value immersive military simulation. Whether you're an experienced Arma veteran or new to the game, we provide training and opportunities to develop your skills in a supportive environment.

Our Principles

  • Realistic tactics and procedures
  • Clear chain of command
  • Professional communication
  • Supportive learning environment
  • Regular operations and training sessions

Mission Types

STG conducts a variety of mission types to keep operations fresh and challenging. Our dedicated mission makers create immersive scenarios that test our tactical abilities.

Combat Operations

Direct action missions focused on eliminating hostile forces, securing objectives, and establishing control in contested areas.

Reconnaissance

Intelligence gathering operations with minimal engagement. Focus on stealth, observation, and reporting enemy movements and positions.

Search and Rescue

High-risk operations to locate and extract friendly forces or civilians from hostile territory.

Weekly Schedule

We conduct operations every Friday at 2000 GMT and training sessions every Wednesday at 1900 GMT. Additional pickup games and smaller operations occur throughout the week.
